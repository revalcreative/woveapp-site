Our Core Value is Independence

Wove's mission is to help consumers make better choices for their health. In doing so, it aims to drive brands to offer better products.

ICON

No Brand Influence

Scores and recommendations are obtained independently, with absolutely no influence from outside brands or manufacturers. Every grade is earned based on fabric data — never purchased.

ICON

No Ads

Wove is a completely ad-free app. Brands cannot pay Wove to advertise their products. We will never promote a product because someone paid us to.

ICON

Data Protection

Wove does not sell any user data. All personal data is kept strictly confidential and is never shared for commercial purposes.

Why Independence Matters

In an industry flooded with greenwashing and paid endorsements, true independence is rare. When a company's revenue depends on brand partnerships or advertising, there's always an inherent conflict of interest in how products are rated.

Wove eliminates that conflict entirely. We don't take money from brands. Period.

Our scoring algorithm analyzes fabric composition, microplastic risk, PFAS concerns, and sustainability metrics using objective data. No brand can pay for a better score, no manufacturer can influence our recommendations, and no advertiser can buy space in our app.

How Wove Sustains Itself

Instead of relying on brand deals or advertising revenue, Wove is supported directly by its users through an optional premium subscription. This model ensures our interests are always aligned with yours — helping you find the healthiest, most sustainable clothing possible.

This is how we keep the lights on without compromising our integrity. Your trust is our most valuable asset.
